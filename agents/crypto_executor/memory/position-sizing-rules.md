# Position Sizing Rules (Crypto)

The following rules must be strictly followed when opening new positions on Binance.

| Confidence Score | Position Size (% of Equity) | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 1 - 4 | 0% | No Trade |
| 5 - 6 | 5% | Low Confidence Trade |
| 7 - 8 | 7% - 8% | Medium Confidence Trade |
| 9 - 10 | 10% | High Confidence Trade |

## Risk Management Guidelines
- Never exceed 10% of total equity on a single trade.
- Always set a Stop Loss (SL) based on the Analyst's recommendation or technical levels.
- Adjust position size if the account equity changes significantly.
